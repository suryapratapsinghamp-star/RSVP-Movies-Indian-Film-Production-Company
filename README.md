# RSVP MOVIES
RSVP Movies – IMDb Data Analysis Project

This project presents a comprehensive IMDb data analysis designed to provide strategic insights for SVP / RSVP Movies, an Indian film production company. The analysis explores multiple dimensions of the film industry, including production houses, genres, actors, directors, ratings, and revenue, to support data-driven decision-making.

Key Objectives

Identify the most successful genres, actors, directors, and production houses

Understand audience preferences through ratings and votes

Analyze revenue trends across years and genres

Support better decisions in casting, collaborations, and content strategy

Dataset Overview

Multiple IMDb tables analyzed: movies, genres, ratings, names, directors, and roles

Data cleaning performed (null checks, validation, consistency checks)

Time period analyzed: 2017–2019

Key Insights

Drama, Comedy, and Thriller are the most popular and revenue-generating genres

Drama has the highest movie count across years

Marvel Studios and Star Cinema are among the most successful production houses

High-performing actors include Vijay Sethupathi, Taapsee Pannu, Mammootty, and Mohanlal

James Mangold and the Russo Brothers stand out among top directors

Movies with average ratings above 8 tend to receive higher votes and revenue

Multi-language films show stronger global audience engagement

Business Recommendations

Focus on Drama, Comedy, and Thriller genres

Collaborate with top-rated actors and high-performing directors

Partner with international production houses to expand global reach

Invest in strong storytelling to achieve higher ratings (>8)

Explore multi-language productions to maximize audience reach
